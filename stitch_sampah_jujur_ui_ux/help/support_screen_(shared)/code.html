Frequently Asked Questions

General Pickup Payment
How do I schedule a pickup? expand_more

To schedule a pickup, go to the 'Requests' tab and tap the '+' button. Select the materials you want to recycle, choose a pickup date and time, and confirm your request.

What materials can I recycle? expand_more

We accept a wide range of materials including paper, cardboard, plastics (types 1 & 2), glass bottles and jars, and aluminum and steel cans. Please check the app for a detailed list.

How do I get paid? expand_more

Payment is processed through the app after your collected materials are weighed and valued. You can link your bank account or digital wallet for seamless transfers.
